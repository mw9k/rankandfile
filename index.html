About

  • This training game helps to improve your recognition of chessboard coordinates - important for understanding chess theory.
  • How To Play: Follow the dot and choose the correct coordinates from the options listed below the board.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: Use ASD or 123 to make a multiple choice selection.
  • No Peeking… For best learning, players should try to formulate their guess before looking at the available options.
  • Recommended strategy: start in File Only or Rank Only mode, at first using the "Show Quadrants" aid. As you improve, try "File & Rank" mode, with fewer pieces visible, and even flip the board.
  • No data collected. Settings and scores are saved in your browser's localStorage, similar to cookies.
  • Requires a modern browser. Designed for mobile - plays best on a touchscreen device.
