About

  • This training game helps improve quick recognition of chessboard coordinates; a useful skill for understanding chess theory.

  • How To Play

    Identify the coordinates of the animated circle, choosing between the 3 options listed below the board. Act fast before the circle's timer runs out.

  • Keyboard Shortcuts

    Use ASD or 123 to make a multiple choice selection.
    Use + & - to adjust the board size to suit your screen.

  • No Peeking

    To get the most from the game, try to formulate your guess before looking at the available options.

  • Recommended Strategy

    Start out in File Only or Rank Only mode to build up intuition. The "Show Quadrants" aid may be helpful at first.

  • Privacy

    Settings & high scores are automatically saved in your browser's localStorage. No user data is tracked or collected.

  • Designed for Mobile

    Plays best on a touchscreen device.
    Requires a modern browser.
