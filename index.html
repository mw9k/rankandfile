About

  • This training game helps to improve your recognition of chessboard coordinates - important for understanding chess theory.
  • To play, follow the dot and choose the correct coordinates from the options listed below the board.
  • Ideally, players should try to formulate their guess before looking at the available options.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts:Use A-S-D or 1-2-3 to make a multiple choice selection.
  • The site was designed especially for mobile. Tapping the multiple choice options is easiest on a touchscreen device.
  • Recommended strategy: start in File Only or Rank Only mode, at first using the "Show Quadrants" aid. As you improve, try File & Rank mode, with fewer pieces visible, and even flip the board.
  • Settings are saved using localStorage; similar to cookies. Only the settings and high score are saved; there is no tracking etc.
  • Requires a modern browser.
